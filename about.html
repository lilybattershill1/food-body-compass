About this tool

Created by two people who believe that evidence-based support should be accessible in the hardest moments.

VP
Vania Phitidis
Registered Intuitive Eating Counsellor and Psychotherapist

Vania spent decades at war with her body. Beginning at the age of eight, she was caught in a cycle of restriction, bingeing, and relentless self-criticism — convinced the problem was her willpower, her appetite, or simply herself. It took years of deep personal work to realise that the problem was never her; it was a culture that taught her her body was something to be fixed.

That personal journey is the foundation of her professional mission. Today, Vania is a Registered Intuitive Eating Counsellor, a certified MB-EAT teacher, and a BACP-registered counsellor and psychotherapist. Her background includes a B.A. in Psychology, a Humanistic Creative Counselling Diploma, and 30+ years of coaching experience supporting individuals in "coming home" to themselves.

Vania brings a Health At Every Size (HAES), weight-inclusive approach to everything she does. She is clear about her purpose: she isn't here to help clients lose weight. She is here to help them stop fighting themselves — with food, with their bodies, and with their lives. Vania is the psychology brains behind Food and Body Compass!

LB
Dr. Lily Battershill
Mental Health Scientist & Product Lead

Lily is a mental health scientist and advocate dedicated to the intersection of data-driven technology and clinical care. Her passion for this work started with lived experience of binge eating and bulimia.

Her background spans high-impact frontline and systemic roles, including volunteer work with Crisis Text Line and research at ieso Digital Health and Limbic. A voice for lived experience, Lily delivered a TEDx talk titled "Changing from the Inside Out" — sharing her journey of bulimia recovery to challenge societal standards of success.

Beyond her clinical and data work, Lily is a qualified yoga and meditation teacher. She has been contributing her technical and product skillset to Food & Body Compass.

About Food & Body Compass

Food & Body Compass is a free, evidence-based toolkit designed for difficult moments around food and the body. Every resource — the guided audio, worksheets, and step-by-step guides — is grounded in CBT, intuitive eating research, and self-compassion science.

It is not a replacement for professional support. If you are struggling, please reach out to a qualified therapist or one of the helplines listed on the support page.
