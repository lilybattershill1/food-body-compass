After a binge — recovery

The episode is over. What happens now matters more than what just did.

What's going on

The hours after a binge are when the harshest self-talk tends to arrive. This is also when the most important recovery choices happen — how you treat yourself now shapes the pattern going forward.

Try this now
1

Avoid restriction: skipping meals after a binge often fuels the next one.1

2

Be deliberate about self-compassion — speak to yourself as you would a close friend.2

3

Do something gentle and non-food-related to re-regulate (walk, shower, call someone).

4

Use the reflection sheet when you feel calm — not as punishment, but as understanding.
