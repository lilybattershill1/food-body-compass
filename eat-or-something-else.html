Not sure if I need to eat or need something else

When you're reaching for food but something feels off about the impulse.

What's going on

This is one of the most useful questions you can learn to sit with: am I physically hungry, or am I reaching for food because I'm bored, stressed, lonely, tired, or numb? Both are valid — but the response is different.

Try this now
1

Pause before eating and do a quick body scan: where do you feel sensation?

2

Rate your physical hunger 1–10. Below a 4 or 5 often means something else is going on.1

3

Ask: if food wasn't available right now, what else might I reach for or need?

4

Name what you're actually feeling — even roughly — and consider what might genuinely help with that.
