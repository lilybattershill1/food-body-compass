Feeling overwhelmed

When everything feels like too much and you don't know where to start.

What's going on

Overwhelm often isn't about having too much to do — it's about losing contact with what actually matters.1 When everything feels equally urgent, nothing gets done, and the emotional weight builds. The antidote is usually not doing more — it's getting clearer.

Try this now
1

Notice where you feel it in your body — chest, shoulders, jaw. That's where the overwhelm lives. Just name it.

2

Take three slow breaths before you do anything else. Literally nothing else first.

3

Ask yourself: what is the one thing that would make today feel okay? Not the most urgent — the most meaningful.

4

Use the Overwhelm Sorter worksheet below to get everything out of your head and into a clear plan.
