Feeling the urge to binge

The pull is strong but you haven't started yet. That gap matters.

What's going on

An urge is a wave — it builds, peaks, and passes.1 You're at the point where there's still space to respond rather than react. That space is powerful, even if it doesn't feel like it.

Try this now
1

Notice the urge without acting on it: "I notice I want to binge right now."

2

Ask: what am I feeling underneath this? (stressed, lonely, numb, anxious?)2

3

Start with the intro audio below, then let the main session follow on.

4

If the urge passes, notice what helped — that's useful for next time.
